03 · Pain & bottleneck

Pain — the symptom

Capable agents sit unused. Teams won't let an agent move money, because one wrong, irreversible action — and a regulator asks why.

Bottleneck — the root cause

Agents inherit broad, standing credentials with no per-action permission model — no scoped least-privilege, no human checkpoint, no audit. There's no safe way to delegate a single action.

Our solution attacks the bottleneck — a permission model for actions, not just identities.
